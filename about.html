About Me

I’m a senior HCI student at Your University who treats every design brief like a quest log. My toolkit spans Figma, React, Unity, and eye‑tracking research. I believe great interfaces should be as intuitive as a favourite game controller: low‑friction, deeply engaging, and inclusive for all players.

Beyond pixels, you’ll find me speed‑running indie titles, moderating local game‑jam meet‑ups, and mentoring first‑year designers on accessibility best practices.
